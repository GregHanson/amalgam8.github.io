Why Use Amalgam8?

Amalgam8 simplifies microservice integration tasks and enables you to focus on the DevOps functions such as canary testing, A/B testing, red/black deployments, systematic resilience testing, etc.

You can use the Amalgam8 platform with popular container runtimes like Kubernetes, Marathon/ Mesos, VMs, or your favorite cloud provider such as IBM Bluemix, Google Cloud, Amazon AWS, etc.
